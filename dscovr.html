DSCOVR - Deep Space Climate Observatory

DSCOVR is a satellite designed to monitor space weather and Earth's climate. Launched in 2015, it orbits the Lagrange point 1 (L1), about 1.5 million km from Earth, where it can observe solar wind and provide early warnings of geomagnetic storms.
