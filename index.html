What Happens Next?

When these particles hit Earth, they cause something called a Geomagnetic Storm. Earth has a force field called the magnetosphere, which protects us. But when the Sun's energy hits our force field, it shakes things up!

Imagine Earth wearing an invisible shield! The storm from the Sun causes that shield to wobble like jelly.

Additionally, these geomagnetic storms can disrupt satellite operations, communication systems, and even power grids on Earth. Scientists continuously monitor solar activity to predict and mitigate these effects.
